Chapter 1
The glowing white eyes. The blurry screenshots. The red-circled YouTube thumbnails yelling “REAL PROOF!!” That was our language. It didn’t matter if it was fake. we were all in on it. Over time, the art shifted: spooky Herobrine turned into meme Herobrine, comic-book Herobrine, even hero Herobrine. He became whatever we wanted him to be.
